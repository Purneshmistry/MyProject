ABOUT US

Meal.com is startup in the food catering industry with the vision of “to inspire working people to choose a healthier, hygiene and more compassionate lifestyle through the home food eating and committed to create stronger, healthier Humboldt community”. In the fast paced life of the city, we are often pressed for time and consequently skipping meals or eating calories loaded junk food.

Meal.com delivers the hygienic, healthier home food for you to save your time with delicious food with homemade quality.
